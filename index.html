Delicious Chettinad food

Chettinad is a region located in the southern indian state of Tamilnadu. The term is used to refer to 96 villages where Natukottai Chettiars originate from. Chettinad is famous world-over for its unique cuisine and architecture and has great importance historically and culturally.The Chettinad region is located 90 km from Madurai, Tamil Nadu, with a semi-arid area of 1,550 sq km. It is occupied by the chettiars or chetti families, who belong to a wealthy clan of traders and financiers who amassed their wealth by trading in Southeast Asia during the 19th century. This region represents authentic architecture, known for its artistic work with a mix of traditional Indian architecture and a touch of European influence.
